## Style Prompt

A premium technical product film for VideoMaking Studio Workspace 2.0: dark editorial canvas, warm devotional saffron accents, precise teal signal lines, and cinematic product-system motion. The video should feel like a serious AI media operations console, not a generic SaaS ad.

## Colors

- Background: `#070806` near-black with a slight warm cast
- Panel: `#101411` deep graphite green-black
- Foreground: `#F5F1E6` warm ivory
- Muted text: `#B8B09C` quiet parchment gray
- Primary accent: `#F2A23A` saffron/orange for key actions
- Signal accent: `#28C7B7` teal for AI/data flow
- Success accent: `#81D67A` green for completion

## Typography

- Display: `Fraunces`, heavy and editorial for large declarations
- Data/body: `IBM Plex Mono`, lightweight for pipeline labels, metrics, and system readouts

## What NOT to Do

- No purple-blue gradients, neon cyberpunk palette, or generic AI sparkle overload
- No rounded marketing cards stacked everywhere
- No tiny web-sized text; the video must read at a glance
- No jump cuts; every scene change should have a visible transition
- No default black/white, Roboto, Inter, or generic SaaS typography
